📜 3MatesCraft – Official Rules

🛡️ Gameplay Rules

  • No hacked clients
  • No movement mods
  • No inventory mods
  • No health indicators
  • No radar or minimap mods
  • No freecam
  • No ESP
  • No auto / easy place mods
  • No macros or scripts
  • No auto clickers
  • No mouse tweaks / scroll mods
  • No crafting modifications
  • No abusing bugs or glitches
  • No attempting to duplicate items
  • No duplicating items
  • No IRL trading
  • No invite reward systems
  • No external gambling
  • No Discord boost rewards for in-game benefits
  • No cross-server trading
  • No staff impersonation
  • Maximum of 5 accounts per player
  • No finding or using the world seed
  • No spamming voice chat
  • No griefing, raiding, or stealing — respect all builds and claims
  • Report all bugs, glitches, and cheaters to staff

💬 Chat Rules

  • No spamming or encouraging others to spam
  • No harassment or toxic behavior
  • No advertising or promotion
  • No discrimination or hate speech
  • No threats of any kind
  • No sharing others' private information
  • No pretending to be staff members
  • No ban evasion
  • No low-effort or spammy forum posts
  • Use common sense — just because something isn’t listed doesn’t mean it’s allowed
⚠️ Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, mutes, bans, or permanent removal.

Play fair, respect others, and enjoy your time on 3MatesCraft!
